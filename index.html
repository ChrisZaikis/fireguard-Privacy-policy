Privacy Policy – FireGuard

Updated: 5ctober 2025

FireGuard (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates as a mobile application designed to provide real-time wildfire alerts, fire-risk levels, and weather-based safety recommendations.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Location data – used only to provide localized fire-risk and weather alerts.
  • Device information – Android version, device ID, region, and language.
  • Crash reports – to improve performance and reliability.

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information such as names, emails, or phone numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide accurate wildfire alerts and weather information.
  • Improve and optimize the app experience.
  • Deliver safety notifications based on your current area.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is processed securely using encryption and trusted APIs (NASA FIRMS, OpenWeather). Location data is processed locally and not stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party APIs:

  • NASA FIRMS API – wildfire detection data
  • OpenWeather API – weather and wind data

Each of these services has its own privacy policy.

5. User Rights

You may disable location access in your device settings at any time or request data deletion by contacting us.

Contact: christos.zaikis@gmail.com

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page.
